Home

Luminous Bison is a campus pop‑up that rents portable study pods: foldable desk shells with an integrated LED task light and noise‑damp headphones. Reserve by the hour, tap to unlock, and settle in anywhere—library, atrium, even outdoors.

We also offer compact Productivity Kits with everything you actually use: fine‑point pens, sticky tabs, index cards, a 25‑minute timer, and a palm‑sized lamp. No clutter, no gimmicks—just the essentials to get you across the finish line.

Rent a Pod

Hourly rentals with student pricing. Cleaned between uses. Optional noise‑damp set and privacy visor.

Productivity Kits

Choose Focus, Exam, or All‑Nighter kits. Refillable pieces so you don’t buy what you already have.
