About

We noticed the same problem every exam week: all the good spots are gone, and the ones that remain are noisy or poorly lit. Luminous Bison started as a simple solution—bring the quiet, light, and focus with you.

Our pods fold flat, roll on a small cart, and set up in under two minutes. We clean and maintain the gear, and sell refills for your kit so you keep costs low. It’s small, sustainable, and built for students.
